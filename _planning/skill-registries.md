## Our Strategy

**Publish to:** SKILL.md format → distribute to SkillsMP, ClawHub, skills.sh, agentskill.sh simultaneously. One format, everywhere.

**Track daily:** ClawHub, skills.sh, SkillsMP, PulseMCP for new creative/design/agency skills.

**Build the gap:** Nobody curates this for creative shops. We become THE registry for design shop workflows.

**Monetize later:** Agent37 model — sell premium skills via Stripe once catalog is proven.
